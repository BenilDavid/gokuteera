    About us

    Gokuteera is a family run enterprise with a passion for all that is food! We take pride in adopting the correct techniques and practices and aim for long term sustenance over short term profits.We aim for Gokuteera to showcase the principles we stand for. We’ve done things the right way and will continue to do so.

    Gokuteera is prepared by people who care, for people who care. We believe our practices should increase sustainability and minimise our carbon footprint. We have modernised our processes to help us achieve this goal. We believe in transparency and that everyone should know where our food comes from. That’s why we make our products on the farm and keep our milk as local as we can.
